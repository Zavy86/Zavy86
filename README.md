#### Glad to see you here!

I'm an information technology science enthusiast who turned years of self-education and freelancing into a career as a software developer.

A few years ago I aimed to become a Full-Stack Developer, but I soon realized that it is impossible to be a genius in everything, however the curiosity to focus on every technology makes me able to sketch out any MVP from scratch.

But if I had to choose the area in which I perform best, it is definitely the creation of Backends that do all the heavy lifting for your app or website; I love designing systems that are light yet powerful, distributed yet synchronized and beautiful yet effective.

I also have a YouTube channel where I talk about software development, system design, databases, servers, optimization, and general purpose technologies from behind the scenes.
